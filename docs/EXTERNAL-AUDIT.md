Ceitnot underwent an **independent smart contract audit** by [Contract Wolf](https://contractwolf.io/). This page lists public artefacts. For the same links in table form plus token-report notes, see also [CONTRACT-WOLF-AUDIT.md](./CONTRACT-WOLF-AUDIT.md).

## Important

- A third-party audit **does not replace** your own code review and is **not** investment advice.  
- Reports refer to **specific contract versions** and certificate dates — reconcile them with the commit or deployment you rely on.

## Product UI

The Ceitnot web app shows Contract Wolf links and the badge in the **site footer** and on the **Security** page (`/security`).
